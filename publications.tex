\section{Publications Plan}\label{sec:publications}
This sections outlines the papers that have already been published or that are planned to be published soon as follows:
\begin{itemize}
	\item \gls{siuv}: a policy-based and context-aware identity and privilege management for smart cars. This refers to the work described in Section \ref{sec:siuv}. It has already been published in IFIP Sec 2021 \cite{siuv}.
	\item Per-authorisation state models for \gls{ucon}: This refers to the work described in Section \ref{sec:dynamic_dfas}. This is still a work in progress in the last stages and it is planned to be published in Euro S\&P 2022\footnote{https://www.ieee-security.org/TC/EuroSP2022/} (deadline: September 23, 2021). Other possible candidate conferences include: IEEE S\&P 2022\footnote{https://www.ieee-security.org/TC/SP2022/}, AsiaCCS 2022\footnote{https://asiaccs2022.conferenceservice.jp/}, or CODASPY 2022\footnote{http://www.codaspy.org/2022/cfp.html}.

	\item A policy-based middleware for GAIA-X \gls{iam}: This refers to Section \ref{sec:gaiax} and is planned to be published in ESORICS 2022 (deadline: typically in January). Other candidate conferences: IFIP Sec 2022 or ARES 2022.
